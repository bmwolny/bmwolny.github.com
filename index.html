This site is a launchpage for my Clio 2 coursework History 697: Creating History in New Media. On my blog you can find posts relating both to this class and History 696, Clio Wired.

What is this all about?

Creating history online is much more than just text on a screen! Any historian should have a basic understanding of the digital tools available and how to use them. Hopefully my classmates and I will have that basic understanding by the end of the semester, and have a little fun along the way.

Who am I?

I am a history PhD Student at George Mason University. My major field is American history with minor fields in military history and public history. My research interests include women in the military.
